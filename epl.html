Ending Cards

Riev, the Blood Beryl
His faith shaken twice, Riev decided to take matters into his own hands, and he created his own religion. Before long, it would become the regnant faith of Lyon's new Magvel. Its primary tenets were acceptance and tolerance.

Selena Fluorspar
Selena fought at the helm of Lyon's armies as his leading conqueror. Before long, people all around the world began to loathe her. She was unconcerned. She had long since learned to silence the screams of her heart's regrets...

Glen, the Sunstone
Filled with contempt for life itself, Glen knelt before the Demon King without a moment's hesitation. His was among the first of many deaths the Demon King would be responsible for in Magvel's darkest era...

Caellach Tiger Eye
Seeing the writing on the wall, Caellach stole a ship and sailed away from Magvel, never to return. Months later, he came upon an uncharted, scarcely populated continent. He went on to become the land's first king.

The Moonstone
Freed from the shackles of humanity, Valter terrorized the land, killing countless innocents to quench his unsatiable bloodlust. He would eventually be destroyed by a mage after attacking the wrong village.

Medusa, Petrous Countess
Medusa returned to the underworld, where she remained eerily quiet for a time. She later started a war that would rock the underworld's very foundations, vying for total dominion.

The Moonstone & Medusa, Petrous Countess
Medusa brought Valter to the underworld, where she inducted him into her coven. Together, they later started a war that would rock the foundations of the underworld, vying for total dominion.

Beran, the Hunter
Beran remained in the army of Grado, seeking worthy challenges. Few records remain of him.

Cantankerous Breguet
Breguet stayed in the army of Grado. He would tell anybody within earshot all about how superior he was to all of his peers in the army. He was killed by a stray arrow in a skirmish with bandits.

O'Neill, True Warrior

Fomortiis Route

O'Neill set out on a journey of self-discovery, seeking to find the warrior inside him. Or so he said. In truth, he was just terrified of the Demon King. He never returned to Grado.

Lyon Route

O'Neill set out on a journey of self-discovery, seeking to find the warrior inside him. Or so he said. In truth, he was disgusted by his new emperor's actions. He never returned to Grado.

The Mighty Bazba
Bazba tried to gather the remnants of Gerik's mercenaries into his own band. After receiving refusal after refusal, he wrote a book on the unsung woes of leadership. People were shocked he knew how to write.

Bone, Meek Bandit
Bone was too small a man for the whirlwind that followed the War of the Stones. Terrified for his life, he attempted to run away. He would find his end mere hours later, torn apart by the monsters chasing him.

Zabba, Vengeful Sellsword

Fomortiis Route

Zabba continued to work as a mercenary under the Demon King. As the fighting grew fiercer, her will to live decreased. During a battle, she got distracted thinking of her old friend. She paid for this error with her life.

Lyon Route

Zabba continued to work as a mercenary in Grado's payroll. As the fighting grew fiercer, her will to live decreased. During a certain battle, she got distracted thinking of her old friend. She paid for this error with her life.

Bone, Meek Bandit & Zabba, Vengeful Sellsword
Zabba and Bone escaped the Grado army when nobody was paying them attention. They spent their days hence exploring ruins and dungeons. They found happiness getting lost in the past, unworried by the dark future.

Lieselotte, Dark Messenger
Satisfied with the experience she had gained during the war, Lieselott left the army. Little is known of her activities afterwards, though it would seem hers was a life steeped in blood and violence.

Aias, Great Strategist

Fomortiis Route

Aias left Grado's army and became one of the top tacticians opposing the Demon King. His name became known and respected throughout the world, until the day he was struck down by the Demon King himself.

Lyon Route

Aias remained in Grado's army, where he became one of its top tacticians. His name became known and feared throughout the world, until the day he was poisoned by a traitorous subordinate.

Aias, Great Strategist & Lieselotte, Dark Messenger
The two remained in the army and led Grado's only pegasus division together, until the day Aias gave his life to save Lieselott. In his final moments, Aias finally understood his mother's feelings.

Stalwart Saar

His loyalty for Grado unwavering, Saar climbed the ranks until he happened to discover the truth of Emperor Vigarde's death. He loudly announced he would make it known. He was swiftly silenced.

Lyon Route

Tiffani remained in Lyon's tutelage until she became of age and was inducted into his personal guard. As one of the most powerful imperial magicians, she quickly became feared across the realm.
